## Automated daily AI intelligence

Keeping up with the pace of AI development is a full-time job. I automated it.

**What it does:**

A scheduled task runs daily and generates a curated brief of AI news, model releases, tool updates, and notable research. The briefs are delivered to my Obsidian vault and available through my AI agent setup.

**Why I built this:**

The AI space moves fast enough that missing a week can mean missing a significant tool release or architectural pattern that's directly relevant to my work. Rather than checking multiple sources manually, I have an agent that does the research, filters for relevance, and presents a concise summary.

**Stack:** Claude agents, scheduled tasks, Obsidian integration
